Finding Req Practice Problems

Test your understanding with these interactive practice problems.

Practice Problems

Problem 1

Find the equivalent resistance of two resistors in series, with resistances of 4Ω and 6Ω.

Solution: For resistors in series: Req = R1 + R2 = 4Ω + 6Ω = 10Ω

Problem 2

Find the equivalent resistance of two resistors in parallel, with resistances of 4Ω and 6Ω.

Solution: For resistors in parallel: Req = (R1 × R2) / (R1 + R2) = (4Ω × 6Ω) / (4Ω + 6Ω) = 24Ω / 10Ω = 2.4Ω

Problem 3

Find the equivalent resistance of a combination of resistors in series and parallel.

Solution: First, find the equivalent resistance of the parallel resistors, then add it to the series resistor.
