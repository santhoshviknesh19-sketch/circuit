Mastering Equivalent Resistance

Simplify messy circuits into one resistor – you'll wonder how you lived without this!

What's Equivalent Resistance?

It's your shortcut to tame complex resistor setups. Turn a bunch of resistors into one with the same overall effect – genius for calculations!

Series: just add 'em up. Parallel: flip and add reciprocals. Easy peasy.

The Formulas You Need

Series: R_eq = R1 + R2 + ...

Parallel: 1/R_eq = 1/R1 + 1/R2 + ...
