Capacitance Course

WELCOME TO THE CAPACITANCE COURSE!

Course Overview

In this course, you will learn about capacitance, which is a fundamental property of capacitors in electronics that describes their ability to store electrical charge. By the end of this course, you will be able to calculate capacitance values, understand capacitor combinations, and apply these concepts to real-world circuit applications.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Capacitance: Understanding the basic concepts of capacitors, charge storage, and capacitance.
  • Mathematical Formulation: Learning the formula C = Q/V and related equations for energy and time constants.
  • Capacitor Combinations: Applying series and parallel capacitor formulas to find equivalent capacitance.
  • Practical Applications: Exploring how capacitors are used in filtering, timing, and energy storage applications.
