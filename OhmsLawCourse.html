Ohm's Law Course

WELCOME TO THE OHM'S LAW COURSE!

Course Overview

In this course, you will learn about Ohm's Law, which is a fundamental principle in electronics that describes the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance. By the end of this course, you will be able to apply Ohm's Law to solve various circuit problems and understand how it is used in real-world applications.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Ohm's Law: Understanding the basic concepts of voltage, current, and resistance.
  • Mathematical Formulation: Learning the formula V = I * R and how to rearrange it to find voltage, current, or resistance.
  • Practical Applications: Applying Ohm's Law to analyze simple circuits and solve for unknown values.
  • Advanced Concepts: Exploring power calculations and how Ohm's Law relates to other circuit laws.
