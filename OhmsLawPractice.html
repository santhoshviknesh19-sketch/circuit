Ohm's Law Practice Problems

Test your understanding with these interactive practice problems.

Practice Problems

Problem 1

A circuit has a voltage of 12V and a resistance of 4Ω. What is the current flowing through the circuit?

Solution: Using Ohm's Law: I = V/R = 12V / 4Ω = 3A

Problem 2

If a resistor has a current of 2A flowing through it and a voltage of 10V across it, what is the resistance?

Solution: Using Ohm's Law: R = V/I = 10V / 2A = 5Ω

Problem 3

What voltage is needed to produce a current of 0.5A through a 20Ω resistor?

Solution: Using Ohm's Law: V = I × R = 0.5A × 20Ω = 10V
