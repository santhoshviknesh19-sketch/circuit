Circuit Simulator

Build circuits in the canvas below. Drag components, connect them with wires, and experiment freely.

Start Building

1) Pick a tool (Resistor, Battery, or Lightbulb) and click the canvas to place it.
2) Switch to Wire mode, then click two components to connect them.
3) Add nodes or connect wires to create branching/parallel paths.
4) Click “Simulate Circuit” to see a quick result.
5) Double‑click any component to edit its value (supports units like 10k or 9V).
