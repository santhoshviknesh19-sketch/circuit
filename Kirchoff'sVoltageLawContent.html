Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL)

Voltages around any loop add to zero – energy conservation in action!

KVL Basics

Go around a loop: rises and drops cancel out. Sum V = 0

Your Formula

V1 + V2 + V3... = 0 (rise +, drop -)

Steps to Solve

1. Find loops.

2. Assume currents.

3. Sum voltages = 0.

4. Crunch numbers.

Example

12V battery, 4Ω & 2Ω drops: 12 - 4I - 2I = 0 → I=2A!
