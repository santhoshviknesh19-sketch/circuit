Ohm's Law Course

Master the fundamental relationship between voltage, current, and resistance.

Course Content

  • What is Ohm's Law? - It defines the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance in an electrical circuit.
  • Mathematical Formulation - The formula V = IR relates voltage (V), current (I), and resistance (R).
  • Units of Measurement - the units used for voltage (volts), current (amperes), and resistance (ohms).
