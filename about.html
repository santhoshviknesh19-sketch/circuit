About Us

Hey! We're four students who turned our hackathon project into something awesome for learning electronics.

📖 How It All Started

We met at a hackathon and wanted to create something fun and educational. We all liked building things and working with wires and stuff so we decided to make a website that teaches electronics in a way that's actually enjoyable. We wanted to make it easy for anyone to learn, whether you're a total newbie or just want to brush up on your skills.

It's all about real feedback from students like you. We're constantly adding new stuff and fixing what doesn't work.

🎯 Why We Do This

We want everyone to love electronics, not just engineering majors. From zero knowledge to building cool projects – that's our goal! Whether you're tinkering for fun or studying seriously, we've got you covered.
