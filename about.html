About Circuit

Four students, one hackathon, and a passion for making electronics accessible to everyone.

📖 Our Story

Circuit was born during our school's annual hackathon—a place where creative ideas come to life. Four students from different backgrounds came together with a shared vision: to make electrical engineering accessible and engaging for everyone. What started as a challenge quickly evolved into something we were truly passionate about.

We realized that many students struggle with electronics because available resources are either too simple or too complex, with little room for hands-on, interactive learning. So we built Circuit—a comprehensive platform that combines interactive tutorials, practical projects, and cutting-edge simulation tools. Every feature we develop is driven by real feedback from students and educators.

🎯 Our Mission

At Circuit, we believe that electrical engineering and electronics should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to break down complex concepts into digestible, interactive lessons that inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.

We're passionate about making learning engaging, practical, and fun. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to deepen your knowledge, Circuit is here to guide you through the fascinating world of electronics.

🚀 What We're Building

Circuit combines multiple learning approaches to help you master electronics:

  • Interactive Courses - Learn fundamental concepts like Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's Laws, and capacitance
  • Hands-on Simulator - Design and test circuits in real-time with our advanced circuit simulator
  • Practice Problems - Solve real-world problems and reinforce your understanding
  • Best Practices - Develop proper circuit design habits and troubleshooting skills
